IExtensionDeviceGetSupportedCultures Method

[This is preliminary documentation and is subject to change.]

Retrieves all cultues supported by this device.

The format of the returned strings are "languagecode2-country/regioncode2" as defined by the ISO 639-1 standard and the ISO 3166 standard.

For example, "en-US" for English in the United States.
