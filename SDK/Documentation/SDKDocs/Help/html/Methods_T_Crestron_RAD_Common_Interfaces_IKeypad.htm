IKeypad Methods

[This is preliminary documentation and is subject to change.]

The IKeypad type exposes the following members.

Methods
  NameDescription
Asterisk
Method to send a "*" to the Device.
Dash
Method to send a "-" to the Device.
Enter
Method to send the enter command to the Device. Typically, Enter is used to force a channel number change
KeypadBackSpace
Method to send a Back Space to the Device.
KeypadNumber
Sends a keypard number to the device.
Period
Method to send a "." to the Device.
Pound
Method to send a "#" to the Device.
SendKeypadString
Method to send a series of keypad characters to the device.
